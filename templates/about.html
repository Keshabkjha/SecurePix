ℹ️ About SecurePix

SecurePix is a powerful AI-driven encryption platform that secures your images with cutting-edge chaotic maps and intelligent classification.

  • 🔐 End-to-end encryption using chaotic systems: Logistic, Circle, Ikeda
  • 🧠 AI-powered image classification (ResNet50)
  • 📊 Domain detection: Medical, Legal, Surveillance, Personal, Financial
  • 📁 No server storage – your files are never saved
  • ⚡ Real-time encryption/decryption interface
  • 🎨 User-friendly animated UI with responsive design
  • 🚀 Optimized for speed, security, and simplicity
  1. 🖼️ Upload your image securely (drag & drop supported)
  2. 🧬 Choose a chaotic map + custom password
  3. 🔒 Encrypt with one click
  4. 🧠 Auto-classify image domain with ResNet50
  5. ⬇️ Download encrypted image securely
  • 🏥 Hospitals encrypting patient reports & scans
  • 🏛️ Legal firms protecting evidence images
  • 🏢 Enterprises storing confidential project images
  • 📷 Personal privacy protection on the go
  • 🔁 Chaotic encryption ensures each output is unique — even with the same image & password
  • 🧠 Trained on over 10,000 categorized images for AI accuracy
  • ⚡ Encryption completes in under 2 seconds on average
